Instructions

Upload a Photo

  • Click "Upload" to upload a photo.

Select the Destination Area

  • We need to select an area in the photo to "draw" onto.
  • Click anywhere on the photo to draw the first point. We will draw a total of four.
  • ALWAYS START WITH THE TOP LEFT AND MOVE COUNTER-CLOCKWISE. In this example, we start with the top-left corner of the painting and move counter-clockwise...
  • Bottom-left comes next...
  • Then the bottom-right...
  • ...And finally the top-right.
  • When we draw all four points, you will notice two new features on the photo. You will see a grid in the area where you drew the four points, and also a new box to drag around...

Select the Area to be Captured

  • We will use the clear black box that appears to select the area that we want to capture.
  • We can resize it...
  • ...and drag it around.
  • To get the infinty effect, make sure the clear black box and the grid overlap.

Transform and Download

  • Once we have the clear black box in place, we hit the "Transform" button. The new image will appear on the right.
  • Click "Download" to save the new image, or click "Reset" to try again!
